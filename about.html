About us

We provide the solution to the thousands of helpless girls who have no idea on how to get help after an abuse or how to prevent getting abused.
Our ultimate goal is to be a beacon of hope to vulnerable and abused women, to protect and defend them while watching them blossom as butterflies out of their cocoons.

Cocoon of hope provides a system of support, education and advocacy for sexually abused women especially those in under-served areas in Lagos. We offer resources on
prevention and surviving sexual abuse while providing 24hours live chat to victims of sexual abuse

Thousands of girls face various forms of abuse daily and have no one to speak up for them, defend them or direct them towards help.
We are the link between victims and those who can help; we connect victims to our various volunteer professionals or instutions willing to be of help.
We also organise sensitisation programs on the requests made on our webpage by schools, religious bodies and camps, this sensitisation programs are geared towards educating male and female folks alike on the dangers of rape, how to avoid rape and the steps to take immediately after sexual abuse occurs.
We regularly update our list of help hotlines and champion the cause for girls who are willing to prosecute and get justice. Feel free to contact us through the contact form or call 08036502237. for further information
